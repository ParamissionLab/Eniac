# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, practical, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about risks or mistakes.
- Keep discussions relevant to Eniac, agent behavior, documentation quality, and safe engineering practice.
- Respect privacy and do not share private information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.
- Encouraging unsafe use of agents, tools, credentials, or user data.
- Repeated behavior that maintainers have asked you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, limit participation, or block users who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the maintainers at [ParamissionLab](https://github.com/ParamissionLab). Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is adapted from common open source community standards and tailored for this project.
